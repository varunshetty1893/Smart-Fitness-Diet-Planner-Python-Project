About Diet Planner

Your smart companion for healthy living

Diet Planner is a user-friendly web application designed to help individuals monitor their Body Mass Index (BMI), track daily calorie intake, and maintain proper hydration levels. Our platform simplifies health tracking and provides easy-to-understand insights.

Our Mission

We aim to make health monitoring simple, accessible, and effective for everyone by combining smart calculations with a clean dashboard experience.

Core Features
  • Accurate BMI calculation
  • Personalized calorie recommendations
  • Water intake tracking
  • Interactive health dashboard
  • Secure login system
