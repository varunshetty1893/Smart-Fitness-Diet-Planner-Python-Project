Why Is Hydration Calculated Using Weight?

Your body requires water based on your body weight. Larger body mass needs more hydration to maintain proper circulation, digestion, temperature control, and overall health.

Daily Water Requirement = Weight (kg) × 0.033

Example: If your weight is 70 kg → You need approximately 2.31 Liters of water per day.
